Banaue Rice Terraces

Banaue Rice Terraces is located in the mountainside of Ifugao that was built by indigenous Ifugao people 2,000 years ago. It is considered to be the 8th Natural Wonder of the World by many Filipinos. Visitors can explore the terraces, hike around the mountains, and experience the local culture.

Mayon Volcano

Mayon Volcano is located in Albay, Bicol. It is known for its perfect symmetry and iconic cone shape. It erupted many times already, the most recent eruption of it occurred last 2018. Its fertile volcanic soil helps our ecosystem, as the volcano itself serves as a source of geothermal energy.

Rizal Park

Rizal Park or also known as Luneta Park is located in Manila. It is named after Jose Rizal, our national hero. It is a large park that features gardens, museums, and open spaces. It was originally planned to be a place for relaxation, thus, it is now serves as a memorial to Rizal.

Taal Volcano

Taal Volcano is located in Batangas. It is considered as the 2nd most active volcano here in our country and it is also known for its iconic crater lake. It has erupted several times, the most recent eruption of it was last 2020.

Chocolate Hills

Chocolate Hills is located in Bohol. It is composed of around 1,776 hills. It displays a landscape that resembles a chocolate-colored landscape. The geological formation of the Chocolate Hills is the result of millions of years of erosion.
